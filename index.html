Our Story

The Birth of Kaladarpan

What began as a small passionate dream among three students has evolved into a vibrant, inclusive community where everyone has the freedom to explore, create, and connect.

01

The Beginning

Inspired by a powerful lecture from Milind Sir, founding trio Dikshant, Atharv & Ganesh started a mission to uncover hidden talent across Nutan College.

02

First Victory

After performing a street play on campus, Kaladarpan competed in Loksatta Lokankika drama competition — and won 1st place!

03

Growing Community

From skits and street plays to short films, Kaladarpan earned recognition in newspapers, TV channels, and performed at PCMC & Talegaon Nagar Palika events.

04

Kaladarpan Academy

Today, it's more than a club — it's a platform for self-expression and artistic growth, open to all aspiring artists beyond college!
